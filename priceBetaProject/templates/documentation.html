How does it work

HOW DOES IT WORK

As a User all you need to do is open an account with Pricebeta, subscribe to our newsletter channel to get updated prices on products across your favorite online stores. visit the site whenever you are in doubt of a certain product’s price or whenever you are looking for a comfortable price that meets your budget all you need to do is type the name of the product in the search bar and the product will pop up with the price it is going for in different online store

Browse categories

Now that you’ve gotten started with Pricebeta you can easily find your choice product by clicking the dropdown arrow on the browse category option, there you’d find numerous options of gadgets and elctronics to pick from.

Select choice product

Select your favorite item and price that meets your budget by clicking on the respective link which will take you to the page to make your purchase, you can also add to cart.

Getting Started

GET STARTED

Visit our website www.pricebeta.com to access our services. We are a service provider in comparing prices of mobile phones, laptop and accessories across numerous online e-commerce stores. so getting started is as easy as reading our policies, term and conditions, privacy and about us, as all of these contain vital information on our business ethics, offers and limitations

Open an account

For a new account click on register and input ypur details, after creation your details will be automatically saved inacase of next visit. If you happen to log out during the first creation of the account you’d need to sign in during the second visit inputting your details.

Compare prices

Compare product prices across platforms by clicking the “price from...” button and user will be automatically redirected to the e-commerce platform that sells the product you can then make your purchase there.
